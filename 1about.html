About Us
SWAPONIX this is a fast, convenient and reliable service for exchanging cryptocurrency. Our users are not required to register or leave personal information. This allows users to avoid identification and financial theft. Here anyone can easily and profitably exchange more than 1200 cryptocurrencies

Our Goal

Usability and benefit for our users is our main goal. We are developing every day and gaining trust in order to become your permanent service for exchanging cryptocurrencies

Sustainability

The system guarantees uninterrupted processing of transactions and exchanges thanks to stable software that eliminates failures and errors.

Full automatisation

In cooperation with us, we ensure high speed of task completion and eliminate the possibility of errors associated with the human factor.

Openness

We are aimed at ensuring transparency in the operation of the service, actively sharing the necessary data with our users and the public.

Team

For operational and high-quality work, we have assembled a team of specialized professionals. Our employees research in the crypto industry and make improvements to the service.
