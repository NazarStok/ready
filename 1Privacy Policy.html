Privacy Policy
This document sets forth the policies regarding the collection, use, retention and disclosure of information collected from visitors to our website. These policies apply to all of our products and services. Our team attaches great importance to the privacy of user data and takes all necessary measures to protect it
1. User identification information
We may collect personal information about users through a variety of methods, including, but not limited to, when users visit our website or use other options, services or resources provided by our site. Users may be asked to provide their email address. However, anonymous use of the site is also permitted. We collect personal information only when users voluntarily provide us with such information. Users have the opportunity to refuse to provide personal data, except in situations where this may prevent their participation in certain events related to the operation of the web resource.
2. Browser Cookies
To improve user experience, our site may use cookies. These small text files are placed on your hard drive by your web browser for the purpose of keeping records and sometimes tracking certain information. You have the ability to set your web browser to disable the use of cookies or to receive warnings when cookies are being sent. It is important to note that if this feature is disabled, some features of the site may function limited or incorrectly. We recommend that you review your browser's security settings for more detailed information on managing cookies.
3. How we use collected information
Swaponix values your privacy and collects personal information from users for specific purposes, including: 1. Improved customer service: The information we collect from you allows us to more effectively respond to your requests and provide high-quality service that meets your needs.

2. Improving the quality of our website: Your feedback plays an important role in our efforts to improve the quality of products and services provided on the Swaponix website.

3. Transaction processing: The information you provide when placing an order is used solely to provide the required services for that order. We strictly maintain confidentiality and do not share your information with third parties, except when necessary to provide relevant services.

4. Sending periodic emails: Your email address may be used to send you information related to your order and to respond to your requests, questions and other requests.

Swaponix adheres to high standards of data security, ensuring the protection and confidentiality of your personal information. We are committed to providing a positive and safe experience on our site for all of our users.
4. Sharing personal Information
We do not sell, trade or rent users personally identifiable information to third parties. For the purposes described above, we may share generic aggregate demographic information not linked to any personal identification information about visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates and advertisers.
5. Agreement with the terms
By using this online resource, you agree to the current policy, as well as the terms and conditions of use of our website. If you do not agree with this policy, we recommend that you refrain from using our web resource. Your continued use of the resource after changes to this policy will be regarded as your consent to these changes. If you have any questions regarding the current Privacy Policy, use of the site, or other aspects related to the web resource, please contact us at the address provided.
6. Privacy Policy Changes
Swaponix reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Once changes are made, we will indicate the date they were introduced at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to check this page regularly to stay informed about changes and our efforts to protect your personal information. You agree and acknowledge your responsibility to periodically check for and review any changes to this Privacy Policy.
